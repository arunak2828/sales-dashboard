## Dashboard Visuals & Insights

### 1. Sum of Total Price by Product Name
**Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart  
**Insight:**  
- Shampoo is the highest revenue-generating product.  
- Notebook and Orange Juice follow closely.  
- Grapes generate the lowest revenue among the listed products.

---

### 2. Sum of Total Price by Product Category
**Chart Type:** Area Chart  
**Insight:**  
- Personal Care contributes the highest total sales.  
- Fruits and Beverages show strong but declining revenue trends.  
- Stationery has the lowest overall contribution.

---

### 3. Sum of Total Price by Branch
**Chart Type:** Bar Chart  
**Insight:**  
- Branch **A** significantly outperforms Branch **B** in total sales.  
- Indicates higher customer volume or stronger product performance in Branch A.

---

### 4. Sum of Total Price by Gender
**Chart Type:** Line Chart  
**Insight:**  
- Male customers generate higher total sales compared to female customers.  
- Suggests differences in purchasing behavior or basket size.

---

### 5. Sum of Total Price by City
**Chart Type:** Donut Chart  
**Insight:**  
- Chicago contributes the largest share of sales.  
- New York and Los Angeles follow with relatively similar proportions.
